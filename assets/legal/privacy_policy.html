Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This is a placeholder privacy policy for Renew Vault. The final policy will describe how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

Renew Vault stores your renewal, document, and family data locally on your device. Optional features such as cloud backup and crash reporting may transmit limited technical data as described in the full policy.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to provide renewal reminders, document organization, and backup features you choose to enable. We do not sell your personal information.

Data Storage & Security

Vault data is stored on your device. Encrypted backups are created only when you initiate a backup. App lock and privacy protection settings help keep your information secure.

Your Choices

  • Enable or disable notifications and crash reporting in Settings.
  • Export or delete your data through backup and restore tools.
  • Contact support with privacy questions at any time.
