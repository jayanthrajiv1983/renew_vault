## Full description

```
Renew Vault — Never miss a renewal again.

Life is full of deadlines you can't afford to forget — insurance renewals, warranty expirations, passport and licence dates, subscriptions, and more. Renew Vault keeps everything in one secure place, with smart reminders so you're always ahead of what's due.

Stay on top of every renewal
Add warranties, insurance policies, documents, subscriptions, and custom renewals. See what's expiring soon on your dashboard and get notified before it's too late.

Scan documents in seconds
Use intelligent OCR to scan passports, driving licences, insurance cards, invoices, and more. Renew Vault detects document types, extracts key details, and lets you review before saving.

Your data, protected
Renew Vault is built for privacy. Encrypt your vault with biometric app lock, create encrypted backups, and restore with integrity checks — so your information stays yours.

Backup with confidence
Create encrypted local backups, upload to Google Drive, view backup history, and restore when you change devices — all without sacrificing security.

Designed for real life
Clean Material Design, light and dark themes, family member tracking, analytics insights, and thoughtful onboarding — so the app feels premium from the first launch.

Perfect for:
• Home & appliance warranties
• Vehicle, health, travel & life insurance
• Passports, licences & ID documents
• Subscriptions & AMC contracts
• Any date you never want to miss

Download Renew Vault today and take control of your renewals — calmly, securely, and on time.
```
