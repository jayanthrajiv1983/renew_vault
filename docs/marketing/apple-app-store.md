## Description

```
Never miss a renewal again.

Renew Vault helps you manage warranties, insurance, subscriptions, and important documents in one secure app — with smart reminders before anything expires.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

• Track renewals, expiry dates, and important documents
• Get timely reminders before policies and documents lapse
• Scan documents with intelligent OCR
• Review extracted fields with confidence scores
• Lock your vault with Face ID or Touch ID
• Create encrypted backups and restore safely
• Upload encrypted backups to Google Drive
• Organize items by category and family member
• View insights and upcoming renewals at a glance

BUILT FOR PRIVACY

Your renewal data is sensitive. Renew Vault uses encryption, biometric protection, and secure backup workflows designed to keep your information private and under your control.

WHY RENEW VAULT

Most people don't forget renewals on purpose — they forget because dates live in emails, drawers, and memory. Renew Vault brings them together in one place, with reminders that actually help.

Start organizing your life today.
```
