Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about privacy, storage, backups, and how Renew Vault works.

Your vault data is stored locally on your device in encrypted form by default. Renew Vault does not sync your items to our servers. Optional Google Drive backup uploads encrypted .rvbackup files only when you initiate a backup.

You can export an encrypted .rvbackup file to your device or share it through apps you choose. You can also back up to a "Renew Vault Backups" folder in your Google Drive account. Backups are user-initiated—they never upload automatically.

OCR runs on your device to help pre-fill fields from a photo or image. It is an assistive feature—always review and edit extracted text before saving. Raw OCR text is not stored; only the fields you confirm are saved.

Renew Vault schedules local notifications on your device based on the renewal dates and reminder settings you configure. Reminders are not sent from our servers. Enable notifications for Renew Vault in both the app and your device system settings.

No. You can use the core app without creating an account with us. Google Sign-In is used only if you choose optional Google Drive backup or restore.

Yes. Add family members in the app and assign items to them. Family names, relationships, and optional profile photos are stored locally on your device.

Your vault stays on your device
