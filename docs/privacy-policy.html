Privacy Policy content

This Privacy Policy describes how Renew Vault ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Renew Vault helps you organize renewals, warranties, insurance policies, passports, licenses, subscriptions, tax records, and related documents with reminders, search, categories, attachments, and optional encrypted backup.

We designed Renew Vault so that your vault data stays on your device by default. We do not sell your personal information. Optional features that transmit data off your device—such as anonymous crash reporting and Google Drive backup—are described below and are under your control.

1. Introduction

Renew Vault is a personal organization app. Most information you enter is stored locally on your device in encrypted form. We do not operate a cloud account system for your vault contents, and we do not receive your renewal items, attachments, or family profiles unless you choose to back them up to a service you control (for example, Google Drive) or contact us by email.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide in the App

When you use Renew Vault, you may enter or generate the following data, which is stored locally on your device:

  • Renewal and document records — titles, categories, expiry/renewal dates, notes, and custom metadata (for example, policy numbers, passport details, or warranty information you choose to save).
  • Attachments — photos, images, and PDF files linked to your items.
  • Family members — names, relationships, and optional profile photos you assign to items.
  • App preferences — notification settings, reminder schedules, theme, app lock, backup preferences, and similar settings.
  • OCR-assisted input — when you scan a document, text is recognized on your device to help pre-fill fields. Raw OCR text is not stored; only the structured fields you save and optional correction hints are kept locally.

We do not require you to create an account with us to use the core App.

2.2 Information Stored Locally for App Operation

The App also stores technical and operational data on your device, including:

  • Encrypted local database entries (renewals, family members, settings, and diagnostic logs).
  • Encrypted attachment files.
  • Scheduled local notification identifiers tied to your reminders.
  • Backup history metadata (for example, date and destination of a backup you performed).

This local data is not transmitted to us automatically.

2.3 Anonymous Crash and Error Reports (Optional)

If you opt in to Share Anonymous Crash Reports in Settings → Privacy & Security, the App may send crash and stability information to Firebase Crashlytics (Google) in release builds only. This is disabled by default until you grant consent through the in-app prompt or Settings.

When enabled, reports may include:

  • Exception type and stack trace
  • App version
  • Feature area label (for example, OCR, BACKUP, NOTIFICATIONS)
  • Operation label (for example, a generic failure description)

Crash reports do not include your renewal items, attachments, OCR text, document numbers, file paths, family member details, or other personal vault content. You may turn crash reporting off at any time in Settings.

2.4 Information Sent When You Contact Support

If you use Contact Support or related feedback options, your email app may send a message you compose to us. Pre-filled diagnostic lines may include app version, release channel, device platform, operating system version, theme preference, and timestamp. You control what you ultimately send. We use support correspondence only to respond to your request.

2.5 What We Do Not Collect

Renew Vault does not use Firebase Analytics or similar third-party usage analytics. In-app charts and statistics (for example, items expiring soon) are computed on your device and are not sent to us.

We do not collect precise location data, contacts, SMS, call logs, or advertising identifiers for tracking purposes.

4. Local Storage and Encryption

Your data is protected on your device as follows:

  • Encrypted local database — Renewal records, family members, settings, OCR correction hints, and app logs are stored in encrypted Hive databases using AES-256 encryption.
  • Encrypted attachments — Photos and PDFs attached to items are encrypted at rest on your device (AES-256-CBC).
  • Encryption keys — Keys are stored in platform secure storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain) and are not written to plain files or unencrypted preferences.
  • App Lock — Optional biometric or device-credential lock restricts access to the App.
  • Privacy protection — Optional settings can hide App content in the task switcher and show a privacy overlay when the App moves to the background.

When attachments are opened or previewed, temporary decrypted copies may be created in the App's cache and cleared according to system cache behavior.

5. Backup and Restore

Renew Vault offers optional backup and restore tools. Backups are user-initiated unless you configure backup reminders (which only remind you to back up; they do not upload data automatically).

5.1 Local and Shared Backups

You may export an encrypted .rvbackup file to your device storage or share it through your chosen app (for example, email, cloud storage, or messaging). Exports use encrypted .rvbackup format (ZIP payload with AES-256-CBC). Legacy plain .json import may be supported for migration; new exports use encrypted format only.

5.2 Google Drive Backup (Optional)

If you choose Google Drive backup or restore, the App uses Google Sign-In to access a dedicated "Renew Vault Backups" folder in your Google account. Only encrypted .rvbackup files are uploaded or downloaded. We do not operate the Google Drive servers; Google's terms and privacy policy apply to data stored in your Google account.

Google Sign-In is used only for this optional backup feature—not for App login or vault sync.

7. We Do Not Sell Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not use your vault contents for advertising or cross-app profiling.

8. Data Retention

  • On your device — Data remains until you delete items, remove attachments, clear App data, or uninstall the App.
  • Backups you create — Retained wherever you store them (device, Google Drive, email, etc.) until you delete those files.
  • Crash reports — If enabled, retained by Google Firebase Crashlytics according to Google's retention practices. Disabling crash reporting stops new reports from being associated with your continued use (subject to Google's processing of data already received).
  • Support emails — Retained only as long as needed to handle your inquiry and for reasonable record-keeping.

9. Your Rights and Choices

You can control your information as follows:

  • View and edit — Manage renewals, family members, and attachments within the App.
  • Delete — Delete individual items, attachments, or family members in the App. Uninstalling the App removes local App data from your device (subject to your OS behavior).
  • Notifications — Enable or disable reminders in Settings.
  • Crash reporting — Enable or disable Share Anonymous Crash Reports in Settings → Privacy & Security. A one-time consent prompt may appear on first launch or after an upgrade.
  • App Lock & privacy protection — Configure in Settings → Privacy & Security.
  • Backup — Export encrypted backups when you choose; restore only from files you trust.
  • Google Drive — Sign out or delete backup files from your Google Drive account at any time.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have additional rights (for example, access, correction, or deletion). Because vault data is stored locally, you are the primary controller of that data on your device. Contact us for privacy questions (Section 12).

10. Children's Privacy

Renew Vault is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information through support channels, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.
