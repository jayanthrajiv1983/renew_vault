Developer Information

Renew Vault

com.renewvault.app

Renew Vault is a local-first mobile app for tracking warranties, insurance, passports, licences, subscriptions, tax records, and important documents—with reminders, encrypted attachments, optional backup, and on-device OCR.

For technical help and troubleshooting, visit our Support page.

Privacy Enquiries

Questions about how we handle information, permissions, backups, or crash reporting?

Renew Vault is local-first by design. Your vault contents stay on your device—we don't operate a cloud account for your data and we don't sell personal information.

Read our full Privacy Policy for details on collection, encryption, third-party services, and your rights.

Email privacy question

For data-related requests referenced in the Privacy Policy, email jayanthrajiv@gmail.com with subject Renew Vault Privacy Enquiry and describe your request.

Response Times

We aim to respond within 2–3 business days for most inquiries. Complex issues—especially backup restore, data recovery, or reproducible bugs—may take longer while we investigate.

If you haven't heard back after 5 business days, please send a brief follow-up with your original subject line.
